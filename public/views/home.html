02

O.E.E.

O.e.e. is a method to identify and quantify losses in a process. This work helps plants and BUs to prioritize their actions and follow their improvement. It also help to benchmark and lunch projets.

In order to implement o.e.e. we specified, produced guidance, defined governance scheme and develop a tool.

This tool helps to manage data and visualize results. It is multi-language tool with link to hyperviseur to get datas.

image

03

Big data analysis and reporting

Then to handle 98 o.e.e. and millions of data we use a database called JADE. JADE is not able to provide the solution to review results, do business intelligence, reports as we wish...

We then try to find solution to provide an efficient reporting with what we have and we develop a tool based on Excel power pivot to handle large amount of datas and produce high quality reports. Suez report, quartely, monthly review and at all level, from plant to group.
